What we're building

We're developing an AI-centric platform to unify property data, lease administration, rent collection, budgets, and settlements—aiming to reduce operational drag and increase clarity across portfolios.

  • Property master with clean data model
  • Lease lifecycle, invoicing, and delinquencies
  • Common‑cost & utilities budgets with settlements
