What we're building

An AI-centric platform that is the source of truth for all real estate data, facilitating lease administration and property management and financial analytics. AI agents integrate with workflows to resolve issues, manage contracts, and unlock insights.

  • Centralised database for properties, leases, and tenants
  • Digital workflows with automated processes
  • AI agents and IoT integration for predictive analytics

Why XVue?

Real estate operations are fragmented across spreadsheets, legacy systems and error-prone manual processes. XVue brings everything into one intelligent platform.
